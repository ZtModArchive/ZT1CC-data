A recent Arctic expedition has uncovered a startling discovery. In a secluded clearing in the Arctic is a jungle-filled valley. This valley features such strange terrain as volcanoes, steam vents, geysers and tar pits. The Arctic expedition has come to you first because they believe that if there is one place in the world where a variety of dinosaurs could thrive, this is it. You must create at least eight suitable dinosaur exhibits in this wonder of nature.
