Have you ever been to a marine park and seen a marine animal show? You know, the one where the dolphins or sea lions do all sorts of amazing tricks. An exciting new feature in Marine Mania is the ability to create your own marine animal shows.

Please note that if at any time you wish to exit the tutorial, you can click on the Game Options tab (blue disk) on the left side of the game screen and select Main Menu.

Click the OK button to start the tutorial.
