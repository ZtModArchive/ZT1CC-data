Now that you have added the orca ball to its show tank, the orca's Play with Ball trick is available. Each time you add a new show object to a show tank, it makes new tricks available for that animal. Now we will add the newly available Play with Ball trick to the orca's show script.

1. Click the platform of the orca's show tank.
2. Select the trick Play with Ball on the left side of the panel.
3. Click the right arrow to add this trick to the show script.

Click the OK button to continue.
