You can also adjust the frequency of the show performance. More shows will generate more revenue. However, animals prefer the comforts of home and will become unhappy the longer they’re out of their exhibit tanks. Guests will also not like to see their favorite animals overworked and mistreated.

1. Click the Frequency List box and change the show frequency to infrequent.

This is a good balance between having enough shows and giving the orca time to rest.

Click the OK button to proceed.
