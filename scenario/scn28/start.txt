Marine Mania: Tutorial 2 teaches you the important elements of creating marine animal shows!

You will learn:
-- How to recognize which animals are show animals.
-- How to adopt a show animal.
-- How to build a show tank.
-- How to place grandstands.
-- How to add tricks to a show animal's script.
-- How to change the show frequency.
-- How to purchase and place show objects.
-- How to add tricks that use show objects to a show animal's script.
-- How to research new tricks.
