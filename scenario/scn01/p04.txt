Your zoo has its first Bengal tiger! Now you need to make sure it's cared for. 

1. On the left side of the gameplay screen, click the Hire Staff button, and then click the zookeeper icon. 
2. Place the cursor over the Bengal tiger's exhibit, and then click to place the zookeeper in it.

Zookeepers will feed animals, clean their exhibits, and provide medical assistance to animals. It is important that you have a sufficient number of zookeepers.

Click the OK button to proceed.
