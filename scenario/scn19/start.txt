Dinosaur Digs: Tutorial 1 will teach you the new features of Zoo Tycoon: Dinosaur Digs.

You will learn:
  --How to filter the game content.
  --How to create an exhibit with electrified fencing.
  --How to adopt dinosaur eggs.
  --How to hire scientists.
  --How to access the Building List.
  --How to access the new research programs.
  --How to show and hide guests, foliage and buildings.
  --How to make a suitable dinosaur exhibit.
  --How to set your dinosaurs loose in your zoo!
  --How to purchase a Dinosaur Recovery Team building.
